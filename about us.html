About CPC Solution

CPC Solution was founded with the vision to deliver unparalleled office equipment solutions across Singapore. Our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes with dependable copier rentals, sales, and maintenance services that streamline operations and boost productivity.

With over 25 years of industry experience, we pride ourselves on personalized customer service, expert knowledge, and a commitment to lasting relationships rather than mere transactions.

Our core values include:

  • Customer Focus: Tailoring solutions that meet unique business needs.
  • Quality: Providing equipment from trusted brands including FujiFilm, Canon, Epson, and more.
  • Reliability: Prompt service and maintenance support with industry-leading response times.
  • Integrity: Transparent pricing and ethical business practices.

At CPC Solution, we believe when your business thrives, so do we. We offer flexible rental plans, expert technical support, and environmental responsibility through secure equipment disposal and relocation services.

Located at 110 Lor 23 Geylang, #05-07 Victory Centre, Singapore 388410, we are your trusted partner for all your office copier and IT product needs.
